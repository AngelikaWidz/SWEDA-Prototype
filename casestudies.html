Business Support

Launching a business from scratch

A local resident came to SWEDA with an idea for a home-baking business but no experience writing a plan or managing finances. Our advisors helped her develop a costed business plan, register the business, and identify a small grant to buy equipment.

From redundancy to running her own salon

After being made redundant, one client used her severance pay and a SWEDA-supported plan to open a small hairdressing salon. Ongoing advice sessions helped her manage cash flow and take on her first booking system.

Getting online for the first time at 68

One client had never used a computer before joining SWEDA's digital skills sessions. Over several one-to-one sessions, she learned to send emails, video call her family, and book GP appointments online.

Employment Support

Returning to work after unemployment

One client came to SWEDA after nearly six months out of work. SWEDA helped them create a new CV, attend Job Club, and access an accredited customer service course with a guaranteed interview, resulting in a full-time role at a national retailer.

Overcoming interview anxiety

A client with strong warehouse experience but a history of unsuccessful interviews worked with a SWEDA advisor on mock interviews and confidence-building. He secured a full-time warehouse role within three weeks.
