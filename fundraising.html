Fundraising | Help Us Make a Difference

Every donation helps us continue supporting individuals, families and communities across Sandwell and the Black Country. Together, we can provide opportunities, build confidence and help people overcome barriers to create brighter futures.

Whether you choose to donate, organise a fundraising event or support us through your workplace, your generosity helps us reach more people who need our services.

Your Support Matters

As a charity, SWEDA relies on funding and the generosity of our supporters to continue delivering life-changing services.

Your support helps us provide free guidance and practical assistance to people looking for work, developing new skills, starting businesses, improving their English or accessing financial advice.

Every contribution, no matter the size, helps us make a lasting difference within our local communities
