Supporting Our Communities

For over 30 years, SWEDA has been supporting individuals, families and communities across Sandwell and the Black Country to overcome barriers, develop new skills and create positive opportunities for the future.

We believe that everyone deserves the chance to achieve their full potential. Whether someone is looking for employment, starting a business, improving their digital skills, learning English or seeking welfare advice, our team provides practical, personalised support every step of the way. Our aim is simple: to inspire and motivate people to achieve their full potential and build greater confidence, independence and opportunity in their lives.

Our Story

Founded in 1989, SWEDA was established to support local communities by providing advice, guidance and opportunities that help people improve their lives.

Over the years, we have grown into a trusted community organisation, delivering a wide range of programmes and services designed to tackle unemployment, digital exclusion, financial hardship and social disadvantage.

Today, we continue to work closely with local authorities, funding partners, community organisations and employers to create lasting positive change across the region.

Our Mission

Inspire, Achieve, Motivate

Our mission is to Inspire, Achieve and Motivate our communities. We do this through providing inclusive, accessible and person-centred support that empowers people to develop their skills, improve their wellbeing and achieve their goals.

We recognise that every person's journey is different. That's why we take the time to understand individual circumstances and provide support at a pace that works for them.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We act with honesty, transparency and accountability in everything we do.
  • Empathy: We listen, understand and respond to the needs of our communities with compassion and respect.
  • Collaboration: We work together with partners, stakeholders and communities to create meaningful impact.
  • Innovation: We embrace new ideas, approaches and technologies to improve our services and outcomes.

Our Case Studies

Returning to work after unemployment

One client came to SWEDA after nearly six months out of work. They had lost their role as a Washroom Technician, had an outdated CV, and were struggling to move forward despite previous experience in customer service and retail.

SWEDA helped them create a new CV, attend Job Club and access an accredited customer service course through Sandwell College, which included a guaranteed interview with Tesco. The client received one-to-one support with their online application and interview preparation.

As a result, they secured a full-time role at Tesco in April 2025. Beyond the job outcome, the client reported renewed confidence, financial stability, and a sense of purpose.

Launching a small business from scratch

A local resident came to SWEDA with an idea for a home-baking business but no experience writing a business plan or managing finances.

Our business advisors worked with her over several sessions to develop a costed plan, register the business, and identify a small grant to buy equipment.

She now supplies three local cafés and is exploring taking on her first member of staff.
